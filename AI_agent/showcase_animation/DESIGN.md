# EnergyPlus Agent Showcase - Global Design Direction

## Style Prompt

Calm technical product storytelling on a light architectural canvas. Use restrained
contrast, thin structural lines, compact cards, and clear semantic color roles. Motion
should feel precise and intentional: confident entrances, readable holds, and smooth
continuity between related scenes.

## Colors

- Canvas: `#edf2f3` - outer application background.
- Scene: `#fbfcfc` - primary presentation surface.
- Ink: `#223036` - titles and primary text.
- Flow teal: `#176c72` - data flow, connectors, review, and interaction.
- Agent blue: `#135d96` - EnergyPlus Agent and AI capability elements.
- Model orange: `#ad571a` - BIM/BEM paths and model outputs.
- Risk red: `#b74443` - pain points, warnings, and broken feedback only.

## Typography

- Keep the existing system sans-serif stack to preserve layout and recording fidelity.
- Titles use strong weight and compact tracking.
- Supporting labels remain short, high-contrast, and readable at the recording viewport.

## Motion

- Each shot follows build, breathe, resolve.
- Entrances start after a short 0.15-0.2 second beat.
- Related shots use a restrained push-crossfade; major section changes use a slightly
  longer transition.
- Avoid bounce and elastic motion. Prefer `power2.out`, `power3.out`, `sine.inOut`, and
  `power2.inOut`.
- The final state of every shot remains readable before the next transition begins.

## What Not To Do

- Do not recolor raster or SVG asset files.
- Do not introduce neon colors, heavy gradients, glass effects, or decorative particles.
- Do not use red for ordinary implementation or knowledge labels.
- Do not make every card enter with the same direction, duration, or easing.
- Do not leave a blank frame between shots.
